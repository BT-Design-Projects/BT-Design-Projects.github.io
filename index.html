Features

Built for multitaskers.

Everything you need to track multiple things at once.

⏱

Multiple Simultaneous Timers

Run as many countdown timers as you need. Each operates completely independently.

✏

Custom Timer Names

Name every timer so you always know what's what — Pasta, Workout, Laundry, or anything else.

📊

Visual Progress Bars

Color-coded countdowns shift from green to yellow to red as time runs out.

⚡

Quick Presets

One-tap buttons for 1, 5, 10, 15, and 30 minutes. No typing required.

🔔

Notifications & Vibration

Get alerted the moment any timer completes, even if the app is in the background.

⏯

Pause, Resume & Reset

Full individual control over every timer. Pause one without touching the others.
